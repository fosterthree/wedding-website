Located just across the river from our little house in Morningside, the Allegheny RiverTrail Park is one of our favorite little slices of Pittsburgh. With an overgrown post-industrial setting and waterfront access, the park is a physical representation of the merger of our two lives.

The park features several amenities:

  • A spacious open-air warehouse for dining and dancing
  • A riverfront bar serving tropical cocktails and oysters
  • Access to the Allegheny River, including a public dock
  • A large playground for those bringing children
  • Plenty of green space for activities and walking trails to explore
